Permits & Requirements

Important: All necessary permits must be provided by the land owner prior to commencement of work.

Site Preparation Requirements

Before work begins, the land owner must identify and clearly mark with marking flags/ribbon all of the following:

  • Property boundaries
  • Utility lines (water, gas, electric, septic, communication)
  • Trees or vegetation to be preserved
  • Structures, fences, or other features to be protected
  • Any other areas or items requiring special attention

Proper marking ensures accurate work and protects valuable property features.
