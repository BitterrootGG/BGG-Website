What Defensible Space Really Means

Defensible space is not about stripping land bare. It’s about reducing ladder fuels, managing density, and improving access so structures have a better chance of surviving a wildfire.

Our Approach

  • Zone 1–3 defensible space strategies
  • Ladder fuel and density reduction
  • Improved emergency and equipment access
  • Methods aligned with Firewise principles

Best Fit For

  • Homes and cabins
  • HOA and shared parcels
  • Rural residential properties
