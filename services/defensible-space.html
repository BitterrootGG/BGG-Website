What Defensible Space Really Is

Defensible space is about reducing fire intensity near structures while keeping your land usable. It’s selective thinning, ladder fuel removal, and access improvement.

Our Approach

  • Zone 1–3 defensible space strategies
  • Ladder fuel and density reduction
  • Improved emergency access
  • Firewise-aligned methods
